Frequently Asked Questions

A virtual internship is a remote work experience where interns work for a company entirely online. ByteWing connects students with real companies globally, providing structured tasks, mentorship, and professional growth — all from anywhere in the world.

Our internships range from 4 weeks to 6 months, depending on the company and role. You can filter by duration when browsing opportunities to find what fits your schedule.

Compensation varies by company and location. Some internships are paid, some offer stipends, and some are unpaid but offer academic credit. All details are clearly listed in each internship posting.

No prior experience is required for most internships. We have opportunities for all levels — from first-year students to recent graduates. Each posting clearly states what skills and background are needed.

ByteWing guarantees internship placement for all qualified applicants through our extensive network of 500+ partner companies. If you meet the eligibility criteria, we ensure you receive a placement within our guaranteed timeframe.
