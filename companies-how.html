How to Hire Talent from Our Platform

Unlock your company's potential with the remote intern advantage in three easy steps.

01

Sign Up & Create Your Profile

Register as a Host Company on our platform. Tell us about your business, the projects you're working on, and the type of intern support you need. Our team reviews your application and gets you approved within 48 hours.

02

Get Matched with Pre-Vetted Interns

Our award-winning AI matching engine scans your project requirements and recommends the best-fit interns from our global talent pool within 72 hours. Review profiles, resumes, and portfolios — then choose or receive curated suggestions.

Use our platform tools to onboard remotely, assign tasks, and track progress. We support both you and your intern throughout the entire program — from kickoff to completion certificate — ensuring a productive collaboration for all involved.
